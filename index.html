JJ FARMING

About Us

JJ FARMING is a FARM that specializes in agricultural products and services. It was established in 2019 by KELETSO MATLAPEKE.It started as a small mixed -crop farm which now has evolved into a trusted agri-input supplier for local farmers.JJ FARMING offers a wide range of products including seeds, fertilizers and pesticides.

THE TEAM

The owner of the farm is the man who established it KELETSO MATLAPEKE,Our products are developed by THABO MOYO and LILLIAN NYANDENI.The farm manager is MIKE NDONDO.The farm has over 90 employees.The farm has won awards for being the best farm of the year and best provincial farm.The farm is protected by MAEMO SECURITY

OUR MISSION

is to provide SOUTH AFRICAN farmers with affordable,reliable agri-input and advice to improve crop health,productivity and food security. .
