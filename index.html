Welcome to JJ Farming

JJ Farming is a farm that specializes in agricultural products and services. It was established in 2019 by Keletso Matlapeke. It started as a small mixed-crop farm which now has evolved into a trusted agri-input supplier for local farmers. JJ Farming offers a wide range of products including seeds, fertilizers and pesticides.
