About JJ Farming

History: Founded in 2019 by Keletso Matlapeke, JJ Farming began as a small mixed-crop farm in the heart of South Africa. With determination and innovation, we expanded into a trusted agri-input supplier serving local and regional farmers.

Our Mission

To provide South African farmers with affordable, reliable agri-inputs and expert advice to improve crop health, productivity, and food security.

Our Vision

To become the most accessible and innovative agricultural partner in Africa, driving sustainable farming and rural prosperity.

Our Goals

  • To be the leading online and physical supplier of farming inputs in the country, connecting farmers to the right products and knowledge 24/7
  • To win many awards
  • To become the best and richest farm in AFRICA

Our Objectives

  • To provide high-quality seeds, fertilizers and pesticides to farmers at competitive prices.
  • To offer expert advice and support to farmers on crop management and pest control.
  • To contribute to the growth and sustainability of the agricultural sector in South Africa.
  • To make sure more farmers are able to find JJ FARMING online and physical stores.
